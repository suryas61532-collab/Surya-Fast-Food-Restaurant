About Us

3 STAR Restaurant is a fast food restaurant serving fresh, delicious meals made with quality ingredients. From juicy burgers and crispy fries to local favourites like vada pav and pani puri, our menu blends global fast food classics with familiar Indian street food flavours.

We focus on quick service without compromising on taste. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite on the go or sitting down with friends and family, our kitchen is open every day, and our team is committed to making every visit a great experience.
